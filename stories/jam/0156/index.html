Di Onggri Krokodail The hungry crocodile di @onggri @krokodail

Christian G.

Wiehan de Jager

Georgette McGlashen

Jamaican Creole

Level 1

Wan taim yu did av wahn wel onggri krokodail.

Once there was a very hungry crocodile.

wan taɪm ju @did @av wan @wel @onggri @krokodail

Im tek taim an kwaiyat wen im sorch fi fuud. An den…

He searched for food slowly and quietly. And then…

im tek taɪm an kwaɪjat @wen im @sorch fi fuːd an @den

Pou! Di krokodail atak!

POW!!! The crocodile strikes!

@pou di @krokodail @atak

Afta dat im beli ful, an im api.

After that he is no longer hungry, and he is happy.

afta dat im beli ful an im api

Til im onggri agen.

Until he gets hungry again.

@til im @onggri agen

Written by: Christian G.
Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Georgette McGlashen
Language: Jamaican Creole
Level 1
