Zama is great! Zama is great! @zama ˈɪz ˈɡɹeɪt

Michael Oguttu

Vusi Malindi

Darshan Soni

English

Level 2

My little brother sleeps very late. I wake early, because I am great!

My little brother sleeps very late. I wake early, because I am great!
