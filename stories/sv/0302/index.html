Eld Fire

Deborah Namugosa, Beatrice Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano

Rob Owen

Emma Zarrat

Swedish

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Titta, eld!

Look, fire!

Eld brinner.

Fire burns.

Eld lagar mat.

Fire cooks.

Eld ger värme.

Fire gives warmth.

Eld ger ljus.

Fire gives light.

Titta, eld!

Look, fire!

Eld är fantastisk.

Fire is wonderful.

Och den är mäktig.

And it is powerful.

Written by: Deborah Namugosa, Beatrice Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano
Illustrated by: Rob Owen
Translated by: Emma Zarrat
Language: Swedish
Level 1
Source: Fire from African Storybook
